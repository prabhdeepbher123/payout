Payout Policy

This Payout Policy outlines how and when sellers receive their earnings from successful transactions on our platform. By using the platform, sellers agree to the terms described below.

1. Payout Process

  • After a sale, sellers can request a payout from their dashboard once the order is marked as Completed.
  • Upon request, a payout entry is generated in our system and enters the Processing stage.
  • Funds are transferred to the seller’s registered UPI ID.

2. Payout Phases

a. Processing

A payout request has been received.
The request is recorded in our system and scheduled for release 5 calendar days after the order is marked as Completed, regardless of when the payout is requested.

b. Completed

The payout has been successfully transferred to the seller’s UPI ID.

c. Disputed (if applicable)

If a dispute is raised before the payout is processed, the payout is paused.
The issue must be resolved before the payout can proceed.
Based on the outcome, the payout may be resumed or canceled.

3. Fees and Deductions

  • Platform Fee: 5% of the order value
  • Payment Gateway Charges: As per Razorpay’s standard rates
  • Net Payout: Order Amount – (Platform Fee + Payment Gateway Charges)

4. Payout Method

  • Transfer Method: UPI
  • Payout Timeline: Within 5 calendar days from the order completion date

5. Important Notes

  • Ensure your UPI ID is valid and active. Incorrect UPI details may result in payout delays or failures.
  • Payouts are processed only on working days. Bank holidays or technical issues may cause delays.
  • The platform reserves the right to delay or withhold payouts in cases of suspected fraud, misuse, or compliance concerns.
  • Tax deductions (e.g., TDS) may apply in accordance with applicable Indian laws.

6. Policy Updates

This policy is subject to change. Sellers will be notified of significant updates via email or in-app notification.
